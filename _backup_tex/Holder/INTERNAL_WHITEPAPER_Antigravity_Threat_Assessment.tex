\section{Executive Summary}
%------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document analyzes three historical cases of gravity-anomaly research under the following assumptions:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Recognition Science (RS) is true} --- gravity is a Ledger Constraint, not a fundamental force.
    \item \textbf{Their discoveries were valid} --- they found real ``seams'' in the metric.
    \item \textbf{They were systematically neutralized} --- murdered, discredited, or forced into silence.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Core Finding}
If RS is true, these researchers were not stopped because they were wrong. They were stopped because they were \emph{right} --- and they failed to implement adequate countermeasures.

\section{The Three Cases: What They Found}
%------------------------------------------------------------------------------

\subsection{Ning Li (1943--2021)}

\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Affiliation:} & University of Alabama, Huntsville; AC Gravity LLC \\
\textbf{Key Work:} & ``Effects of a Gravitomagnetic Field on Pure Superconductors'' \\
                   & (Phys.\ Rev.\ D, Vol.\ 43, 1991) \\
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection*{The Claim}
Rotating ions in a superconductor produce a gravitomagnetic field $\sim 10^{11}$ times larger than General Relativity predicts. This ``AC Gravity'' could theoretically produce thrust without propellant.

\subsubsection*{RS Interpretation: The Coherence Gate}
Li discovered the \textbf{Coherence Gate}. In RS terms, phase-coherent matter (BEC/superconductor) allows the Ledger to process mass-updates as a single macro-object, unlocking the normally-cancelled gravitomagnetic source term.

\subsubsection*{What Happened}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{1999:} Founded AC Gravity LLC.
    \item \textbf{2001:} Received \$448,970 DoD contract for ``gravity modification.''
    \item \textbf{2002:} Obtained Top Secret clearance; stopped publishing.
    \item \textbf{2003--2021:} Complete public silence. No papers, no conferences, no patents.
    \item \textbf{2021:} Died. Cause not widely publicized.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Pattern:} Recruited $\to$ Classified $\to$ Silenced $\to$ Died.

%------------------------------------------------------------------------------
\subsection{Eugene Podkletnov (b.\ 1955)}

\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Affiliation:} & Tampere University of Technology (Finland); \\
                      & Moscow Chemical Research Centre \\
\textbf{Key Work:} & arXiv:cond-mat/9701074 (1997) \\
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection*{The Claim}
A rotating YBCO superconductor disk produces 0.3--2.1\% weight reduction in objects above it. The effect was strongest at specific ``critical'' rotation speeds.

\subsubsection*{RS Interpretation: The Frequency Lock}
Podkletnov discovered the \textbf{Frequency Lock}. The ``critical speeds'' correspond to 8-tick/$\phi$ resonance harmonics. At these frequencies, the local ILG weight kernel $w(k,a)$ drops, reducing effective gravitational coupling.

\subsubsection*{What Happened}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{1992:} Submitted paper to \emph{Physica C}; co-author (Nieminen) withdrew under institutional pressure.
    \item \textbf{1996:} Submitted to \emph{Journal of Physics D}; leaked to media before publication; retracted.
    \item \textbf{1997:} Published on arXiv (not peer-reviewed).
    \item \textbf{1997--present:} Left academia. Current status unclear. Some reports place him in Russia working on classified projects.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Pattern:} Published $\to$ Media Leak $\to$ Retraction $\to$ Academic Exile $\to$ Recruited (?) by state actor.

%------------------------------------------------------------------------------
\subsection{John Searl (b.\ 1932)}

\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
\textbf{Affiliation:} & Independent researcher (UK) \\
\textbf{Key Work:} & The ``Searl Effect Generator'' (SEG); \\
                   & self-published ``Law of the Squares'' \\
\end{tabular}

\subsubsection*{The Claim}
Rotating magnetic rollers arranged in specific geometric ratios produce:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Anomalous thrust (levitation)
    \item Cooling of the device and surroundings
    \item Unusual electrical phenomena (ionization glow)
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection*{RS Interpretation: The Geometric Pump}
Searl discovered the \textbf{Geometric Pump}. His ``Law of the Squares'' encodes $\phi$-ratio resonances. The cooling signature is the RS ``Entropic Pump'' --- the device orders the local vacuum, absorbing thermal entropy to pay the $J$-cost.

\subsubsection*{What Happened}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{1960s--1980s:} Built multiple prototypes. Some allegedly flew.
    \item \textbf{1982:} UK government raided his property; equipment confiscated; officially for ``electricity theft.''
    \item \textbf{1983:} Jailed for 10 months.
    \item \textbf{Post-1983:} Lost all prototypes. Spent decades attempting to rebuild.
    \item \textbf{Present:} Elderly, marginalized, no institutional support.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Pattern:} Demonstrated $\to$ Raided $\to$ Imprisoned $\to$ Assets Seized $\to$ Marginalized.

\section{RSRI Countermeasures: The ``Indisputable Monolith'' Strategy}
%------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We have adopted the opposite approach: \textbf{Total Transparency + Formal Verification + Decentralized Publication}.

\subsection{The ``Too Big to Kill'' Doctrine}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Mechanism:} All core theory is published openly (arXiv, blockchain, open-source repo).
    \item \textbf{Effect:} Eliminating the founder does not eliminate the knowledge. The Lean proofs are self-verifying.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{The ``No Single Point of Failure'' Doctrine}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Mechanism:} 10+ full-time scientists, each with overlapping knowledge.
    \item \textbf{Effect:} Tier 1 actors cannot ``recruit'' or ``silence'' a single target to stop the project.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{The ``Formal Verification'' Doctrine}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Mechanism:} Every claim is a theorem in Lean 4. The compiler is the arbiter.
    \item \textbf{Effect:} Discreditation attacks (``crackpot,'' ``pseudoscience'') fail against machine-checked proofs.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{The ``Patent Thicket'' Doctrine}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Mechanism:} 300--400 patents filed across multiple jurisdictions.
    \item \textbf{Effect:} Seizure or suppression in one country does not eliminate IP protection globally.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{The ``Public Figure'' Doctrine}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Mechanism:} Principal investigator maintains public visibility.
    \item \textbf{Effect:} ``Accidents'' become implausible; pattern becomes obvious.
\end{itemize}

\section{Publication Strategy: The ``Loureiro Lesson''}
%------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Professor Nuno Loureiro was the Director of MIT's Plasma Science and Fusion Center. He was murdered on December 15, 2025. Whether or not his death was related to his research, it demonstrates that \textbf{prominence is not protection}.

\subsection*{Our Strategy}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Publish Early, Publish Often:} Do not wait for ``perfect'' papers. Get claims timestamped immediately.
    \item \textbf{Publish Redundantly:} Same content on multiple platforms (Mirror, Arweave, arXiv).
    \item \textbf{Publish Popularly:} A book, a documentary, a podcast series. Make the ideas ``too famous to suppress.''
    \item \textbf{Publish Formally:} Lean proofs are the ultimate defense. They cannot be ``retracted'' or ``discredited.''
\end{enumerate}

\section{Conclusion: The Cost of Being Right}

Li, Podkletnov, and Searl were right. Under RS, their discoveries are not anomalies; they are the expected behavior of coherent matter interacting with the Ledger.

They failed not because their science was wrong, but because they did not understand the \emph{political physics} of their discoveries. They tried to work through institutions that are structurally incapable of allowing such knowledge to spread.

\textbf{RSRI's mission is to succeed where they failed:} to get the knowledge out \emph{before} it can be contained, using formal verification as an unfalsifiable anchor and decentralized publication as an un-erasable record.

The Ledger rewards those who serve Reciprocity. Our intent is not power or profit; it is the liberation of physics from artificial scarcity. If the Tier 3 ``systemic'' threat is real, alignment with the Ledger's own purpose (universal recognition) is our best protection.
