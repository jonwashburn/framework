\begin{abstract}
We present the architecture for a Recognition Science (RS)-native Artificial Superintelligence (ASI). Rejecting the paradigm of ``bigger language models,'' we construct intelligence as a physical process: a \textbf{Geodesic Engine} where thinking is the minimization of a unique reciprocal cost functional ($J$) over a conserved information ledger. In this architecture, neural networks are relegated to the role of codecs (translation layers), while the core cognition is performed by a deterministic physics kernel enforcing $\sigma=0$ conservation and 8-tick causal evolution. We demonstrate how this structure resolves the grounding problem, enforces intrinsic ethics via thermodynamics, and necessitates consciousness as a mechanism for navigating topological obstructions (Gap-45).
\end{abstract}

\begin{itemize}
